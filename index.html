What is Your Health?



Your Health is a web application dedicated to connecting people with mental issues from around the world.
A place where you can vent out your frustrations through posts, get in touch with people having similar issues, talk with them how they dealt with their problems, or just make regular new friends.

Why choose Your Health?

Well, for three main reasons...


Simplicity

Friendly environment, easy to use no matter the experience.

Versatility

Multi-platform communication method.

Efficency

All your mental health needs in one place.
